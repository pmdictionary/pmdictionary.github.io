Contributing to the Dictionary

New terms & Corrections

We try to keep the terms accurate and up-to-date. If you see a mistake or discover a term you think we should include, feel free to file a ticket for us to review.

You can help us accelerate our release schedule by including reference links for supporting articles or definitions in your tickets.

Organizing Issues

We have Labels set up for:

  • New terms
  • Duplicate terms
  • Inaccurate terms
  • Feature requests
