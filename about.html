About

Acknowledgments

Many of the original definitions were sourced from a spreadsheet of terms maintained by Madalyn Chea.

At the time terms were sourced, the spreadsheet was last-updated in September of 2018.

We are grateful to Madalyn for her initial work in sourcing and maintaining her spreadsheet of terms.

By migrating the terms to a more accessible format, we hope to expand on Madalyn's work and improve the utility and convenience that she laid the groundwork for.

We would also like to thank Indy Nagpal for his excellent blog about Mac dictionaries.
